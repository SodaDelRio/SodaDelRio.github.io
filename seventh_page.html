I based both of these paintings off of Bob Ross paintings: "Towering Peaks" and "Winter Frost" respectively; both The Joy of Painting episodes that feature these paintings are up on YouTube, so I definitely recommend checking them out. I picked these two images because I learned how to paint by watching Bob Ross videos, and these two were some of the first I painted using oil. With the growing trend of using AI to create and sometimes "improve" art, I thought I'd try my hand at it. I used SPADE COCO for these.
