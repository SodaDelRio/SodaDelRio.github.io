Videogames have become huge over the last twenty or so years. It just so happens that I am twenty years old at the time of making this project, and I find it really interesting that most videogames do an amazing job at immersion, or rather, players have this habit of immersing themselves into the games they play. So much so that our whole language changes. The line between videogame main character and player gets really blurry; after all, the player is in control. This relationship between player and game influences players to a degree, and I wanted to illustrate that with this project.
